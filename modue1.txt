# 2.1 Understanding the Difference

Evaluating:
 SecondFragment.kt
    var intent = Intent(Intent.ACTION_VIEW)
    intent.type = "text/giftcards_browse"
    intent.data = Uri.parse("https://appsec.moyix.net/api/index")
    intent.putExtra("User", loggedInUser);
    startActivity(intent)

 ThirdFragment.kt
    var intent = Intent(activity, ProductScrollingActivity::class.java)
    intent.putExtra("User", loggedInUser);
    startActivity(intent)


    1.What are the two types of Intents?
        SecondFragment is an implicit intent to view gift cards.
        ThirdFragment is an explicit intent specific t the application.
    2.Which type is generally more secure?
        ThirdFragment is more secure since it can only be accessed inside the application and no other app can intercept it.
    3.What type of Intent is used in SecondFragment.kt?
        Implicit to display information on the screen.
    4.What type is used in ThirdFragment.kt?
        Explicit.
    5.Which Intent is considered the "proper" implementation?
        ThirdFragment since sensitive information is being passed, but can only be accessed by the application. SecondFragment can be compromised by a malicious application.
    6.What does "proper" even mean in the context of secure Android development?
        Making sure sensitive data is held only inside the sandbox and not available to other applications.
